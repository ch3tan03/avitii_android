How much can I borrow?
Once you have created your profile as a borrower, you can fill in your expenses and income within the budget section. Based on these figures, the system tells you how much money you can afford to borrow from us.
Can I repay my loan early?
You can always pay off your loans earlier and even get a smaller discount if you choose this option.
Does the lender offer refinancing?
Many lenders choose to keep the same lenders. If their terms are good, one can always borrow from the same lender again.
When should I apply?
You can always apply for money on the website as long as you have no debt, but the payment date will always be the next payment date.
Can anyone become a borrower on avitii?
All it takes to become a borrower, is your ability to solve problems in a given domain in addition to having the heart of a good teacher (the ability to explain how problems are solved)
You need to sign up to borrow money?
Once you have created your profile and filled it out, you can apply for money. Remember to fill it all out to be able to get the best deals, the more you tell about yourself, the cheaper loans you can apply for.
What types of loans can one apply for?
You can search for different loans on the website. You can choose to make your own application or you can search for money on a lender application. We have divided them into several groups such as normal loans, business loans, investment loans, etc.
Why should I use Avitii lending?
Avitii-lending offers an alternative to other loan options, here you have the opportunity to borrow from individuals and companies. Avitii offers a platform that is open to everyone and can be used by everyone.
What is the interest rate?
Avitii-lending works to ensure that there is freedom to enter into the agreements that you want, so as a borrower, you need to look for the lender who has the best terms to get the best loans. We do not interfere in the agreements between two partners, but we set some guidelines that everyone must follow to provide the best possible security.
How do I repay my loan?
Once you have taken out a loan on the website, you sign a contract with the lender, this agreement is binding and can be used in court if the agreement is not kept. 1-3 days before the payment date, you will receive an e-mail or text message with instructions on how and which account you should pay your loan to. Avitii-lending does not receive or borrow money, everything goes between the users of the website.
